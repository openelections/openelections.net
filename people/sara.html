OpenElections People :: Sara Schnadt

Sara is the Project Manager for OpenElections.

Sara Schnadt is a technology project developer, information architect, data visualization designer and new media artist. She has worked as Director of Communications and Outreach at 18th Street Arts Center in Los Angeles, and founder and Chief Technologist for the Chicago Artists Resource (CAR) website at the Chicago Department of Cultural Affairs. At CAR she oversaw software design and development, content design and management and the development of a large online community of contributors. She has also served on the planning Committee for Carnegie Mellon University’s Technology in the Arts Conference, as Marketing Co-Chair for Illinois Arts Alliance’s One State Together in the Arts Conference, and as co-chair for the Chicago chapter of Upgrade!, an international network of new media artists.

In her art practice she works in new media, installation and performance art. She combines data visualization with sculpture and live gestures to describe technology innovation, resulting changes in information access, and their cultural implications. Recent shows include: 12×12: New Artists New Work and Without You I Am Nothing: Interactions at the Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago; Spatial City: An Architecture of Idealism at Museum of Contemporary Art Detroit; Travelogue at The Torrance Art Museum; Upgrade! Beyond Borders at UNESP Gallery, Sao Paulo, Brazil; and the Busan Biennial in South Korea.

More at saraschnadt.com and @SaraSchnadt.
